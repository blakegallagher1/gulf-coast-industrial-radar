## Selected Concept

The selected concept was **Industrial Project / Permit Signal Agent**, later framed as **Gulf Coast Industrial Radar**.

Original contractor/vendor framing:

> An AI-agent platform that detects industrial project formation before bid day and tells contractors what to do next.

## Final First-Pass Pivot

The user added two rules:

1. The first buyer is real estate investors and developers.
2. Use only free sources for data.

This changed the product from contractor/vendor lead intelligence into investor/developer land and site intelligence.

Current first-pass framing:

> Detect industrial demand and site-control formation early enough to buy, option, entitle, sell, or avoid land before the market prices it in.
