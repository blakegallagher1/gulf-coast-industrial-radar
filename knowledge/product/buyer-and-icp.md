# Buyer And ICP

## First Buyer

The first buyer is **real estate investors and developers** focused on industrial land, sites, corridors, and demand formation across the Gulf Coast.

## Jobs To Be Done

- Find land before industrial demand is broadly known.
- Detect quiet land assembly by project entities or adjacent buyers.
- Monitor industrial corridors for new capex, utility load, permits, incentives, and port activity.
- Decide whether to buy, option, entitle, sell, hold, monitor, or pass.
- Build evidence-backed conviction before committing time or capital.

## User Types

- Industrial land investors.
- Developers pursuing logistics, outdoor storage, laydown, industrial parks, build-to-suit, or heavy industrial support sites.
- CRE owners near plant, refinery, LNG, port, power, or data-center corridors.
- Local operators who understand land but lack systematic source monitoring.

## Later Buyer

Contractors and vendors are a strong second buyer segment. Their likely product is project/vendor opportunity radar: prequalification, EPC tracking, bid monitoring, work-package prediction, and pursuit briefs.
