# Product Requirements

## Must Have

- Map-first experience showing industrial site clusters and corridors.
- Evidence archive for every signal.
- Free/public source ingestion.
- Entity, parcel, site, signal, and project objects.
- Quiet land assembly detection.
- Infrastructure proximity scoring.
- Project-formation score.
- Stage labels.
- Investor/developer recommended actions.
- Weekly investor brief generation.
- Source registry with cost, license, cadence, fields, access method, terms review, and ingestion status.
- Raw document preservation before extraction.
- AI extraction returning structured claims with confidence and source excerpts.

## Should Have

- Watchlists by corridor, parish/county, company, port, project type, acreage threshold, and source type.
- Analyst review workflow for merge, split, dismiss, escalate, watch, and false-positive labeling.
- Explainable AI extraction with quoted source excerpts and confidence.
- Backtest mode for known Gulf Coast projects.
- Entity graph for shell LLCs, sponsors, registered agents, officers, addresses, law firms, lenders, and related buyers.
- Alert detail tabs for summary, map/parcels, signal timeline, entity graph, infrastructure, evidence, actions, and analyst notes.
- Filters by corridor, stage, source, project type, acreage, and confidence.

## Later

- Contractor/vendor opportunity tags.
- CRM export.
- Bid calendar.
- Contact enrichment.
- Satellite or aerial change detection.
- Paid data integrations.

## MVP Acceptance Criteria

- At least 3 launch parishes/counties have parcel/GIS ingestion.
- At least 6 public-source lanes are ingested on a schedule.
- Every signal links to raw source evidence.
- Project formation score is calculated and visible.
- Quiet land assembly detector produces site-cluster alerts.
- Map UI shows clusters, parcels, infrastructure proximity, signal timeline, and recommended action.
- Analyst can dismiss, watch, escalate, merge, and split alerts.
- Backtest report shows lead-time performance across at least 5 known projects.
