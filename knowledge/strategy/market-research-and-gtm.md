- Reddit communities such as `r/RealEstateTechnology`, `r/realestateinvesting`, and `r/CommercialRealEstate`.
- Facebook groups such as Real Estate Investor Network, Texas Real Estate Investing Community, and AI For Real Estate.

- Trade publications and thought-leadership articles.
- Free webinar demonstrating the investor ROI of early project alerts.

The GTM should prioritize evidence-backed case studies and a manual brief before broad paid acquisition.

## Competitive Landscape

The research found no direct Gulf Coast-specific AI alert platform for industrial megaproject formation from public data. Adjacent competitors include:

- CoStar - broad CRE intelligence, listings, comps, and market data; strong incumbent but not positioned around pre-bid industrial formation alerts.
- LightBox - geospatial parcel/tax/environmental data and APIs; potential partner or data vendor later, but not MVP under the public/free constraint.
- Altus Group / Real Capital Analytics - transaction intelligence and institutional CRE data; more post-bid than pre-formation.
- Reonomy, LandVision, brokers, consultants, and internal analyst teams - substitute workflows.

The market gap is not static parcel lookup. It is the combination of project-formation detection, public-source evidence, Gulf Coast specialization, and investor/developer next actions.

## Risks

- Public data may not create a durable moat by itself.
- Incumbents could copy regional alerting once demand is proven.
- LLM hallucinations and weak entity resolution can destroy trust.
- Community interest in generic AI real estate may not convert into niche Gulf industrial subscriptions.
- Infrastructure spending and industrial development cycles may soften.
- Source compatibility and legal/terms constraints can slow automation.

## Validation Thresholds From Research

Ideabrowser suggested several validation thresholds. Treat them as hypotheses:

- 50-100 beta users from Reddit/Facebook/LinkedIn.
- 15-25% trial conversion target.
- 20% pilot conversion target in one execution-plan section.
- Less than 8% churn target.
- Cold traffic conversion above 2% and wallet-test deposits above 0.5%-1%.
- Accuracy or precision target above 92% before relying on automated alerts.

## Recommended Near-Term GTM Test

1. Produce a 5-project backtest showing earliest public signals and lead-time days.
2. Turn the best backtest into a weekly brief sample.
3. Send the sample to 20-30 Gulf Coast investors/developers/brokers for interviews.
4. Ask for paid pilot commitments before building a large UI surface.
5. Keep all claims evidence-backed and local; avoid generic "AI real estate" messaging.
