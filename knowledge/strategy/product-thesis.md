# Product Thesis

Industrial development creates public signals before the market fully understands what is forming. Gulf Coast Industrial Radar turns those fragmented signals into investor/developer intelligence.

## Core Thesis

Large industrial projects leave traces across land, entities, permits, incentives, utility filings, port agendas, public-company disclosures, and local government records before they become obvious.

An AI-agent workflow can monitor those traces, extract facts, connect entities/sites/projects, score confidence, and recommend investor/developer action.

## First Product Promise

Detect industrial demand and site-control formation early enough to buy, option, entitle, sell, or avoid land before the market prices it in.

## First Product Shape

- Map-first radar.
- Evidence-backed site clusters.
- Signal timeline by parcel/site/project.
- Confidence and stage labels.
- Recommended investor/developer action.
- Weekly corridor brief.

## What This Is Not First

This is not initially a generic bid-alert system or contractor lead list. Contractor recommendations come later, after the investor/developer radar has reliable signal extraction, entity resolution, and project-formation scoring.
